    You can use two reading modes:
  • - FlowText mode - our in-house technology specifically designed to simplify presenting large texts word-for-word with adjustable speed. Almost as a teleprompter or autocue but better.
  • - Theses mode - Notes that help you stay on track. Designed for improvised speeches.
